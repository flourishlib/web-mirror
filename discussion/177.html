Hi, I am developping a front end for a database I do not own.

There are no primary keys on some tables.

Thanks !

Unfortunately there are quite a number of places in the ORM that use the primary key. For instance, without a primary key it would not be possible to load an individual record unless there was a unique constraint that doesn't allow NULLs, however in that case you basically have a primary key.

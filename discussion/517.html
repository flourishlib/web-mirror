I am back ! And within couple minutes I found out I need to autoload('fEmail')

instead of working with the fEmail.php directly. 

require_once 'init_roots/init_introemail.php'; //My init settings
__autoload('fEmail');

$fEmail = new fEmail();
$fEmail->addRecipient('email@intronooutro.com');
$fEmail->setFromEmail('email@intronooutro.com');
$fEmail->setSubject('Yoo');
$fEmail->setBody('What is goin on maan');
$fEmail->send();

Call to undefined method fRequest::generatecsrftoken() .. why?

This indicates that you are using a revision of Flourish from before r566 when fRequest::generateCSRFToken() used to be fCRUD::generareRequestToken(). You can see the BackwardsCompatibilityBreak page for more details.

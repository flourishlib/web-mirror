posted by mungiu 10 years ago

Yes, MySQL translates BOOLEAN data types to tinyint(1), so when I detect MySQL schemas, I treat tinyint(1) as BOOLEAN, which is why your numbers were being translated to TRUE. A tinyint(2) or greater, or any other integer will be properly detected.
